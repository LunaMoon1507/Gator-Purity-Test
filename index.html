How is this different from the Rice Purity Test?

Great question! The Gator Purity Test has many of the same questions as the original Rice Purity Test, except we “modernized” and switched up the questions to make them more applicable to today’s students. In addition, we added weights to the questions based on the severity of the act, to make certain acts lower your score more than others. The new Gator Purity Test still has 100 questions, with 100 being the highest score (most pure) and 0 being the lowest (least pure).
